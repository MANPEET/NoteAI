# NoteAI

AI-powered meeting transcript summarizer that turns long conversations into clean, structured insights.

Paste or upload any meeting transcript and get:

- 📝 Concise meeting summaries
- ✅ Extracted action items
- 📌 Key decisions and important points
- 🎯 Organized results without manually reviewing hours of conversations

No meeting bots. No call recordings. No complicated integrations.

Just provide your transcript and let NoteAI extract the intelligence.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔐 User authentication with email/password and Google OAuth
- 🤖 AI-powered transcript summarization
- 📄 Upload or paste meeting transcripts
- 📝 Structured summaries with:
  - Overview
  - Key discussion points
  - Decisions made
  - Action items
- 📊 Dashboard to manage previous summaries
- 🔗 Shareable summary results
- 📈 Usage tracking and limits
- ⚡ Fast and responsive Next.js experience
